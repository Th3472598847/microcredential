The Bronx is a borough of New York City. It's known for Yankee Stadium, the home field of the New York Yankees baseball team. Dating from 1899, the vast Bronx Zoo houses hundreds of species of animals. Nearby, the sprawling New York Botanical Garden features a landmark greenhouse with rainforest and cactus displays. By the Hudson River, Wave Hill is a landscaped public garden with wooded paths and a cultural center.

Price

How expensive is the bronx The Bronx is known as the “most affordable NYC borough” thanks to average home prices that are almost $200,000 below New York City's average.

Benefits of living in the bronx Pros: Affordable housing: The Bronx has significantly lower rent and home prices than other parts of New York City. Diversity: The Bronx is home to people from all over the world. Parking: While there are still some areas of high traffic, finding parking is much easier in the Bronx than in other boroughs
